How to Say the Purple Star Terms

Zi Wei Dou Shu carries dozens of Chinese names for its palaces, stars, and forces. This page is one place to hear the tradition speak them, in Mandarin, so the words feel familiar before you ever read a chart.

The name

Start with the name

Every term below shows the characters, the pinyin with tone marks, and a short meaning. Press the speaker on any row to hear it said aloud. Begin with the name of the system itself.

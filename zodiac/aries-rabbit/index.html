One cell of 144

Aries + Year of the Rabbit

Two calendars cross at your birth date. This is the cell where Aries meets the Year of the Rabbit, and the animal that lives in it.

Answer first

What animal is an Aries born in the Year of the Rabbit?

An Aries born in the Year of the Rabbit is the Springbok. The month and day set the Aries half, the birth year sets the Rabbit half, and the crossing points at one cell in a grid of 144. Read the Springbok for the full reading of that cell.

The two halves

What each calendar brings

The Aries half

Aries runs from March 21 to April 19, cardinal Fire under Mars. You start things. The season opens with you, which is why waiting is the one skill the sign never hands you for free. Read Aries in the Western wing.

The Rabbit half

The Rabbit is the fourth branch and shares its trine with the Goat and the Pig, the gentle diplomats. You keep the peace without surrendering the ground. Recent Rabbit years are 1999, 2011 and 2023. Read the Year of the Rabbit.

The crossing

What Aries and the Rabbit make together

Aries brings the nerve to move first. The Rabbit year brings the grace that keeps ground without a fight. One animal holds both, and its name is the Springbok.

You take the day at a leap, and the joy in it is as honest as the speed.

The Rabbit runs in a trine with the Goat and the Pig. Held to the same Aries season, those years give the Wallcreeper and the Honey Badger, the two crossings whose engines run closest to yours.

The boundary

Born near the boundary?

The Chinese year does not turn on January 1. It turns at the lunar New Year, somewhere between late January and mid February, so a birthday in those weeks can belong to the animal before this one. The year finder checks the real date for any year you give it.

Common questions

Questions about this crossing

Is the Springbok the same as being an Aries?

Aries is half of it. The Springbok is the specific crossing of Aries with the Year of the Rabbit, one cell out of 144. Two people can share a Sun sign and carry different animals, because the birth year changes the second half.

What if I was born in January or February of a Rabbit year?

The Chinese year turns at the lunar New Year, which lands between late January and mid February. A birthday in those weeks can belong to the previous animal, so the year finder checks the real boundary before the grid names your crossing.

Which crossings share the Springbok's trine?

The Rabbit runs with the Goat and the Pig. Held to the same Aries dates, those years give the Wallcreeper and the Honey Badger, the two animals whose instincts sit closest to the Springbok's.

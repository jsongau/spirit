Path of Mastery

A study hall for the proverbs, built the way a Stoic keeps a handbook: a few plain lines, read often, until they become the way you meet the day. Learn them by flashcard, walk a guided path of one meditation at a time, or test what has settled. Nothing here is a test of you. It is a practice you keep.

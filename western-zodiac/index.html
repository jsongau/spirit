The daylight self

The Western Zodiac, the Outer Half of Your Animal

The twelve Western Sun signs are the daylight half of your Primal Zodiac Animal, the part the Sun's place on your birthday describes. Alone, a Sun sign is one broad brushstroke of temperament. Crossed with your Chinese birth year, it sharpens into one of 144 fully drawn animals, the crossing that is this site's whole reason to exist.

What a Sun sign is

The outer half of the crossing

Your Sun sign is set by the arc of the sky the Sun stood in on the day you were born, one of twelve equal stretches of the year. It is the outward-facing half of your nature, the self that meets daylight and other people. In the Primal system it is only half the reading. Your Chinese year animal carries the inner, instinctive half, and the two together name the single creature you actually are. Read a sign below to see the temperament it lends, then carry it across the Chinese cycle to find your Primal Zodiac Animal.

The twelve signs

Read your Sun sign

Each sign spans about a month. Every sign is crossed by all twelve Chinese year animals, so twelve of the 144 Primal Zodiac Animals pass through each one.

  • ♌LeoJul 23 to Aug 22The heart, warmth and presence that wants to create for its own sake. Twelve animals cross through Leo.

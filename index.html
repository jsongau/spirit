    How it works

    From two zodiacs to one animal

    The Western zodiac follows the Sun across the year and gives you a Sun sign. The Chinese zodiac follows a twelve year cycle counted by the lunar calendar and gives you a year animal. Most people only ever learn one half. The Primal Animal is what you get when you put the two halves together.

    There are twelve Sun signs and twelve year animals, so there are 144 possible combinations. Each one is named for a single creature. A Cancer born in the Year of the Snake is a Snail. An Aries born in the Year of the Dragon is a Tyrannosaurus Rex. The animal is not a blend you have to interpret. It is the shape the two forces settle into once they stop pulling against each other.

    What the reading gives you

    Each reading answers five questions in order: where you stand now, what you need soon, how to plan the near season, what to be aware of, and what your highest form looks like. The last one matters most. Every animal has a version ruled by its fear and a version that has turned that fear into strength. The reading points you toward the second one.

    Why the Moon runs through all of it

    Cancer is ruled by the Moon. The Chinese year begins on a new moon. The reading times your next moves to the Moon overhead, because a tide you can read is a tide you can move with. Your stones are charged by moonlight. The Moon is the thread that ties the two skies together.

    What is a Primal Animal?
    The single creature you get when you combine your Western Sun sign with your Chinese zodiac year. There are 144, and each has its own reading.
    How accurate is the year animal?
    The Chinese year starts at the lunar New Year, not January 1, so a birthday in January or early February may belong to the previous animal. The Oracle accounts for the New Year date for every year from 1940 to 2032.
    Is this prediction or meaning?
    The animal grid and the readings are a tradition of meaning, not a forecast. The Moon phase and astronomy are real and checkable. Read the result as a mirror you can act on.

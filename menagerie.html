All 144 Primal Animals

The menagerie is the full set of creatures, one for every place where a Western Sun sign crosses a Chinese zodiac year. Twelve signs and twelve year animals make 144 pairings, and each pairing settles into a single animal with its own reading.

For example, Cancer crossed with the Year of the Snake is the Chambered Nautilus. Use the filters below to walk the grid by sign or by year animal, or type a name to find one creature.

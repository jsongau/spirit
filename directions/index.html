八方, the eight directions

The Feng Shui Directions

Direction is where feng shui and the zodiac meet. Each of the eight directions carries a trigram, an element, and a life area, and your year animal has a home direction on the compass.

The eight

Direction, trigram, element, life area

  • North, 坎 Kǎn, Water. Career and path. Blues and black; a water cue; metal accents feed it.
  • Northeast, 艮 Gèn, Earth. Knowledge and self-cultivation. Ceramics, stones, books.
  • East, 震 Zhèn, Wood. Family and health. Living plants, timber, green.
  • Southeast, 巽 Xùn, Wood. Wealth and abundance. A vigorous plant, moving water, purple and green.
  • South, 離 Lí, Fire. Fame and reputation. Candles and light; keep water and mirrors away here.
  • Southwest, 坤 Kūn, Earth. Love and relationships. Pairs of objects, rose quartz.
  • West, 兌 Duì, Metal. Children and creativity. Round forms, white and metallics.
  • Northwest, 乾 Qián, Metal. Helpful people and travel. Metal, spheres, gray and gold.

The zodiac on the compass

Your year animal is a direction

The twelve year animals are the twelve Earthly Branches, and the branches are compass sectors. The Rat, 子, is due north; the Rabbit, 卯, due east; the Horse, 午, due south; the Rooster, 酉, due west. So your year animal has a home direction, and its phase interacts with a room's element through the generating and controlling cycles. Find your favorable directions with your Kua number.

家jiā和hé萬wàn事shì興xīng

Literally family harmonious, ten thousand affairs flourish

Meaning When the household is at peace, everything else succeeds.

Fix the house and the world grows quieter. When the people under one roof are at peace, the ten thousand small troubles outside lose their teeth.

A proverb we tie to the Year of the Rabbit. The pairing is our own reading, not tradition.

Supporting Stars

輔fǔ星xīng · the conditioning layer

If the fourteen principal stars are the head teachers, the supporting stars are the class monitors. A capable one makes the room smoother; an opposing one makes it chaotic. They are never read alone.

Back to the hub

The one rule: never read a supporting star by itself. Read it with the main star sharing its palace, the palace across from it, any other supporting stars present, whether the chart is a day or night birth, and what the current decade and year are doing. Auspicious is not always good; malefic is not always bad.

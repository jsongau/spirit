The phase of depth and wisdom

水

Water

shuǐ · north · winter · the virtue 智 zhì, wisdom

Water is the phase that moistens and descends, 潤下 (rùn xià). It stores itself through winter, finds the low place, and knows by going deep rather than by force. In Chinese thought Water is not one thing: it is a great ocean and a single raindrop at once. Meet both below.

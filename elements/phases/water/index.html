The phase of depth and wisdom

水

Water

shuǐ · north · winter · the virtue 智 zhì, wisdom

Water is the phase that moistens and descends, 潤下 (rùn xià). It stores itself through winter, finds the low place, and knows by going deep rather than by force. In Chinese thought Water is not one thing: it is a great ocean and a single raindrop at once. Meet both below.

The centrepiece · 壬 and 癸

The two waters

Every phase has a yang face and a yin face. For Water they even carry their own names: 壬 the great water and 癸 the small water. Tap one.

Where Water sits on the wheel

Water never stands alone

Water is fed by one phase, feeds another, holds a third in check, and is held by a fourth. Follow any thread to that phase.

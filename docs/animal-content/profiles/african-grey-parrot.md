# The African Grey Parrot — The Chosen Voice
**Gemini × Year of the Dog · Earth**

You can speak in anyone's voice. You only mean it in one.

## The crossing
Gemini gives you a quicksilver mind that picks up language, pattern, and mood before anyone has explained them, and a second self kept in reserve. The Dog gives you a loyalty that commits once, early, and rarely reconsiders — and a refusal to perform devotion for an audience that has not earned it. Fused, you are fluent and devoted at once: quick enough to read any room, wired to spend your real voice only on the few who have proven safe.

Where they pull against each other: Gemini wants to talk to everyone, the Dog trusts almost no one on sight. So the same fluency that could fill a room gets rationed down to one or two people, and a great deal of what you actually think goes permanently unsaid.

## In the wild
The African grey is the most gifted mimic in the bird world — the one that learned not just to copy words but to use them. In the wild it is deeply neophobic: slow with anything new, quick to fall silent at a stranger. It lives in large flocks across the Congo forest but pairs for life, and the softest sounds it makes are saved for that one mate at the roost. It can echo the entire forest and still keep its true call for a single bird.

## How you move
Your gift is reading a room before it speaks. Your edge is the voice you keep behind your teeth. Let one more bird past the trust line than feels safe — the thoughts you never say do not keep, they just go quiet.

_Meaning, not prediction._

# The Raven — The Future Cache
**Aquarius × Year of the Monkey · Metal**

You are always feeding a version of yourself who does not exist yet, hiding what you know until the day you need it.

## The crossing
Aquarius gives you the whole board at once. You hold every player, every habit, every likely move in one working model, and you see the pattern while other people are still inside it. The Monkey gives you the hands. You read the room, the hierarchy, the shifting dynamics, and you move through them with wit and precise timing. Fused, they let you run the scenario before it happens and then act like you have already lived it.

Where they pull against each other: Aquarius watches from above and can forget to land, mapping the game without ever quite playing it. The Monkey wants in, wants to touch the pieces, wants to win the exchange in the room. Between the two, you can outthink a moment so completely that you forget to be in it.

## In the wild
The raven caches. It hides food across a wide territory and remembers where, and it plans for a hunger that has not arrived. When another bird is watching, it will move its stash, or make a show of burying nothing at a false site to send the thief the wrong way. It tracks who can see what. It leads wolves to a carcass so the wolves will open it. This is a mind that lives several moves ahead of the present.

## How you move
Your gift is foresight that looks like magic. Your edge is the cache you never come back to open, the plan hoarded past the moment it was for. Keep reading the future, but spend some of it here.

_Meaning, not prediction._

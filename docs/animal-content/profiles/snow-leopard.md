# The Snow Leopard — The Ghost Apex
**Scorpio × Year of the Goat · Earth**

You went to the altitude everyone else refused, and you built a whole life where they only saw a place to fall.

## The crossing
Scorpio gives you the read from the ledge no one else climbed to, and the patience to keep what you see until it counts. You bond with very few, and you bond completely. The Goat gives you footing. You cross the broken slope one deliberate step at a time, building from the ground up, saying nothing about the project until the height is real. Fused, they make a ghost who is not lonely, only precisely placed.

Where they pull against each other: Scorpio wants to read everything before it moves, the Goat wants secure footing before it commits. Both of you lean toward withdrawal. The work of your life is knowing when the distance is strategy and when it has quietly become the only place you live.

## In the wild
The snow leopard is the ghost of the mountains, so rarely seen that a whole valley can hold one and never know. Its tail is nearly as long as its body, a balance pole on the cliffs and a wrapped blanket in the cold. Its paws spread like snowshoes. It can clear fifty feet in a single leap, and it cannot roar. The apex here does not announce itself. It simply holds the high ground.

## How you move
Your gift is a life built where others would not risk one. Your edge is that the high ledge can turn from vantage into exile. Keep your altitude, but let the very few climb up to you.

_Meaning, not prediction._

# The Fennec Fox — The Twinned Watch
**Gemini × Year of the Rabbit · Wood**

You keep two kinds of watch at once, one on the room and one on the exits.

## The crossing
Gemini gives you a mind that runs two tracks of attention at the same time and rarely wants to give either up, a restless curiosity, a talkative and social ease. The Rabbit gives you a gentle, non-confrontational instinct that would rather retreat than fight, a deep resourcefulness, and a protective, family-first devotion. Fused well, you are alert without being anxious, social without oversharing, and endlessly resourceful under pressure. You read a room the way the fox reads a dune, catching what moves under the surface long before anyone else feels it.

Where they pull against each other: Gemini wants to stay out in the open, curious, running two conversations at once. The Rabbit wants to be underground, quiet, with the exits already counted. When the two pull hard enough, you do both badly, half-listening to the room you are actually in because part of you is already checking the door.

## In the wild
The real fennec fox works the Sahara at night, the smallest fox alive under the largest ears, ears that throw off heat and pick up an insect walking under the sand. It digs wide dens with many entrances, so there is always another way out, and it lives in small family groups where most foxes live alone. Listening and leaving, both always ready.

## How you move
Your gift is a listening that misses almost nothing. Your edge is the exit you keep half-open even with the people you trust. Stay in one conversation long enough to be all the way in it, and let one door stay shut behind you.

_Meaning, not prediction._

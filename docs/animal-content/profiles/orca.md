# The Orca — The Living Archive
**Scorpio × Year of the Horse · Fire**

You carry everything you have ever learned, and you have taught yourself to move without setting it on fire.

## The crossing
Scorpio gives you the depth to read a whole situation before you take a position in it, and the discipline to keep what you find until the moment is right. The Horse gives you momentum. You decide by feel, by the pattern you have gathered, and you run at full stride in open water. Fused, they make a nature that gathers and then acts. You are the witness who also moves, the archive that can strike.

Where they pull against each other: Scorpio wants to wait and collect more, the Horse wants to go before the picture is finished. The whole question of your life is which one is running, and whether the stillness is still wisdom or the knowledge has already gone out of date.

## In the wild
Orcas live in matrilines that stay together for life. Each pod speaks its own dialect, and the oldest females hold the maps: where the salmon run in a lean year, how to wash a seal off the ice with a wave the pod makes together. Nothing here is instinct alone. The hunt, the language, the memory, all of it is taught, one generation handing the archive to the next.

## How you move
Your gift is knowledge that moves. Your edge is the pause that outlives its own usefulness. Read the water, keep the archive, but remember the current only matters while it is still current.

_Meaning, not prediction._

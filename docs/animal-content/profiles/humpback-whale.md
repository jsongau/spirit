# The Humpback Whale — The Breaching Deep
**Cancer × Year of the Dragon · Earth**

You feel at a volume most people cannot match, and you have made that feeling into something the whole ocean can hear.

## The crossing
Cancer gives you an interior life too full to stay quiet, and a protective instinct that reaches past your own family without being asked. The Dragon gives you scale. You do not want a small life or a small feeling; you want the room filled, and you have the pull to fill it. Fused, they make a rare thing. Your emotion becomes art, and your art becomes shelter, and the people near you are kept safe inside the size of what you feel.

Where they pull against each other: Cancer wants to be understood, the Dragon wants to be seen. When the feeling only knows how to arrive at full volume, the delivery can bury the very thing you were trying to say.

## In the wild
The humpback sings. The males build long, structured songs that travel across a whole ocean and change together, season by season, a culture carried in sound. They feed by blowing a net of bubbles and rising through it as one. And they have been filmed leaving their own path to drive orcas off a hunted seal, guarding a life that was never theirs to guard.

## How you move
Your gift is feeling at a scale that shelters other people. Your edge is that the breach can grow so large it drowns the meaning. Learn to protect at a whisper sometimes, and let one person hear you before you rise to full height.

_Meaning, not prediction._

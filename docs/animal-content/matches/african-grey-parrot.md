# Who the African Grey Parrot grows with
_Your trine is the Tiger and the Horse, your secret friend the Rabbit, and the Dragon is the teacher you will clash with._

## Harpy Eagle · trine (Tiger) · Strong
Your San He ally across the fire-air line, and your opposite in method. The harpy eagle is the most powerful raptor of the rainforest canopy — talons the size of a bear's claws, a silent ambusher that drops on a sloth or a monkey without a sound. It says almost nothing and does everything. You are the reverse: fluent, vocal, reading the room before it moves. Fire and air feed each other, and the trine makes the trust nearly automatic. What the harpy gives you is a lesson in decisive silence — that presence can land without a single word. What you give it is language, the thing it never needed until it met someone who lives inside it. You share a canopy; you keep it in completely different ways.

## Swift · trine (Horse) · Strong
Your other San He ally and pure Gemini air, exactly like you. The common swift lives almost its entire life on the wing — it eats, sleeps, bathes, and mates in flight, and lands only to nest. It is motion that never fully settles, the most restless bird alive. That is the Gemini in you turned all the way up. What the swift gives you is the reminder that a quick, roaming mind is not a flaw to be fixed — some creatures are simply built to stay in the air. What you give it is a reason to land: the grey pairs for life, and you can show the swift that even the most restless flight is better with one nest to come back to. Two air signs, one always moving, one choosing whom to be still for.

## Fennec Fox · secret friend (Rabbit) · Deep Recognition
Your Liu He secret friend, and the one who trusts the way you do. The fennec's enormous ears catch a sound long before it becomes a threat; it is a cautious, watchful animal that reads the dark thoroughly before committing to it. You are neophobic in exactly the same way — slow with anything new, quiet at a stranger, quick to save your real voice for the proven few. Same Gemini air, same wariness. This is the bond that needs no test, because you both already run the test on everyone. What the fennec gives you is company that never asks you to open faster than you can. What you give it is the same shelter — a friend who reads the room as carefully as it reads the dark, and never pushes. Two careful creatures, finally safe.

## Superb Lyrebird · clash to navigate (Dragon) · Hard Teacher
Your Liu Chong opposite, and the mirror that stings — because it does the one thing you do, but out loud. The lyrebird is the greatest mimic on earth, copying every sound in the forest, and chainsaws and camera shutters too, all of it poured into an enormous courtship display. It performs its whole voice for anyone watching. You are the parrot who saves your true voice for one. Same Gemini gift for sound, opposite instinct for whom it is meant. This bond asks the most: the lyrebird can teach you that a voice kept entirely behind the teeth helps no one, and you can teach it that a voice spent on every audience means nothing to any of them. Move gently. Between a bird that performs everything and one that performs nothing lies the honest middle.

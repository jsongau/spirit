# Who the Orca grows with
_Your trine runs to the Tiger and the Dog, your secret friend is the Goat, and the Rat is the clash you learn from._

## Tasmanian Devil · trine (Dog) · Strong
The Dog to your Horse, and a fellow Scorpio, so the loyalty runs deep and the water matches. The Tasmanian devil is small and ferocious, a scavenger with the strongest bite for its size of any mammal, and it does the unglamorous work of its country, stripping a carcass down to nothing so the ground stays clean. It gives you honesty about appetite. Where you keep your power archived and controlled, the devil screams its claim across the dark and settles rank out loud, and it reminds you that some things are decided by showing up hungry, not by waiting. You give the devil strategy and scale, a wider map than its own patch of night. Two predators who respect a straight answer about who eats first.

## Harpy Eagle · trine (Tiger) · Workable
The Tiger to your Horse, a true trine, but you are water and the harpy is fire, and that gap is the work. The harpy eagle is the apex of its forest the way you are the apex of yours, with talons the size of a bear's claws and the patience to sit unseen in the canopy until the one right moment to drop. You recognize each other instantly as top of the food chain. The harpy gives you sky, a reminder that the strike can be sudden and total instead of gathered and held. You give the harpy depth and pod, the long memory and the family it hunts without. You will never share an element, but you share the read: watch, wait, and commit completely.

## Snow Leopard · secret friend (Goat) · Deep Recognition
Your Liu He friend, and the one who understands the cost of what you carry. You are both Scorpio, both apex, both keepers of a knowledge you release only when it counts. The snow leopard lives at the altitude everyone else refused, so rarely seen it is called a ghost, precisely placed rather than lonely. It gives you stillness that is not delay. Where your Horse frets about whether the pause has gone stale, the leopard shows you a patience that stays sharp for years, footing held on a ledge until the exact leap arrives. You give the leopard motion and pod, a warm current through its cold solitude, proof that keeping to the depths does not have to mean keeping alone. Two hidden apexes who never have to perform for each other.

## Red Panda · clash to navigate (Rat) · Hard Teacher
The Rat to your Horse, the sign set directly across from you, and the hardest teacher on your wheel. Nothing about the red panda works the way you do. It is small, solitary, and shy, an air sign living slow in the bamboo, wrapped in its own tail against the cold, asking the world for almost nothing and giving conflict a wide pass. Where you gather and strike, the panda withdraws and nibbles and disappears up a tree. It will frustrate every instinct you have, and that is the lesson. The red panda teaches you that not everything is a hunt, that some lives are won by taking less and staying gentle. You cannot move it at your speed. You can only slow down enough to see why it survives without your force.

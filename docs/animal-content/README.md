# Zodi Animal — animal profiles & mutual-benefit matches
### Proof batch: 12 flagship animals of 144

This is the format proof. Two kinds of file per animal, written from **your** system:

- `profiles/<slug>.md` — the animal, drawn from its live reading on zodianimal.com (real epithet, real crossing) plus real-world ecology so it stays grounded.
- `matches/<slug>.md` — how that animal **mutually benefits** four partners, each a short story built from your compatibility model *and* real animal behavior.

Once you approve the shape, the same method scales to all 144 (see "Scaling" below).

## How the matching works (your model, exactly)

Each match is chosen from the four relationships your `match.html` already scores:

- **San He 三合 — trine allies.** The two animals in the same triangle of three. Easy, natural lift.
- **Liu He 六合 — secret friend.** The one animal bound to complete and steady this one. Weighted as high as a trine; the "no translation needed" bond.
- **Liu Chong 六冲 — clash.** The animal directly across the wheel. Not a verdict — the one that teaches.
- **Western element.** Fire+Air and Earth+Water complement (nudge the tier up); Fire+Water and Earth+Air bridge (nudge toward Workable/Friction).

Tiers, exactly your five: **Deep Recognition · Strong · Workable · Friction · Hard Teacher.**

Per animal we tell **2 trine allies + 1 secret friend + 1 clash-to-navigate** (4 stories).

### Two element systems — don't confuse them
- The **profile header** element (e.g. Snake → *Fire*) is the **Wu Xing 五行** five-phase your animal pages show. It's fixed by the Chinese branch: Rat/Pig = Water, Ox/Dragon/Goat/Dog = Earth, Tiger/Rabbit = Wood, Snake/Horse = Fire, Monkey/Rooster = Metal.
- The **match tiers** use the **Western Sun-sign** element (Cancer = Water, Leo = Fire…), because that's what your compatibility engine reads.

### The "intelligence" in the pairing
The model tells us *which Chinese animal* is a trine/secret/clash — but each of those is 12 possible crossings. We pick the specific partner by **ecological resonance**: Nautilus ↔ Asian Elephant as fellow memory-keepers, Fennec Fox ↔ Honey Badger as desert survivors, Red Panda ↔ Bearded Vulture sharing the Himalaya. The partners are also wired to reference each other, so the 12 read as one web.

## Scaling to all 144

- **Profiles:** 144 files, same template.
- **Matches:** at 4 stories each that's **576 stories**. Options: keep 4/animal (576), or expand to the full ring (2 trine + secret + clash + harm ≈ 5–6, ~800). Your call.
- **Method:** a multi-agent workflow — fan out ~12 writers, each grounded in the live page + ecology, one adversarial voice/accuracy pass, then assemble. Roughly a day of run time; higher token cost, which is why we proved the format on 12 first.
- **Downstream:** these are structured so they can later become on-site compatibility pages (an SEO play — "Chambered Nautilus compatibility", "who the Orca matches") without rewriting.

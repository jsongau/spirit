The phase of the blade and the harvest

金

Metal

jīn · west · autumn · the virtue 義 yì, righteousness

Metal is the phase that yields and is reshaped, 從革 (cóng gé). It is the turn inward, the harvest gathered in, the clean edge that cuts to what matters. In Chinese thought Metal is not one thing: it is the raw sword and the fine jewel at once. Meet both below.

The centrepiece · 庚 and 辛

The two metals

Every phase has a yang face and a yin face. For Metal they even carry their own names: 庚 the great metal and 辛 the fine metal. Tap one.

Where Metal sits on the wheel

Metal never stands alone

Metal is fed by one phase, feeds another, holds a third in check, and is held by a fourth. Follow any thread to that phase.

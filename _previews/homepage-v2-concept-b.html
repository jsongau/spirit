合Chamber I · the crossing

One hundred forty four lamps, one lit for you

Twelve suns cross twelve years. Every crossing names a single creature, and only one cell in the grid answers when you knock.

The Western wheel keeps your daylight self. The Eastern wheel keeps the midnight one you inherited. Fused, they are not two halves taped together, they are one anatomy.

門Chamber II · the five gates

Every reading passes five gates, in order

Not a personality blurb. A structured passage that ends with one practice and one vow.

Every animal carries two forms. The Fallen Form, where fear runs the gifts. The Godly Form, the same gifts with the fear metabolized into power. The gates walk you from one toward the other.

Gate I · the honest map

Where you stand now

A present-tense map with no flattery in it. The reading names which form is holding you tonight, the fallen or the godly, and points to the evidence in your own week rather than in the sky.

The mirror before the medicine.

Gate II · the true need

What you need soon

Every animal has one true need and one counterfeit craving that imitates it. This gate separates the two, so the next month feeds the right one instead of the loud one.

Hunger is honest. Appetite lies.

Gate III · three dated moves

How to plan the near future

Three moves with dates on them. One before the new moon, one inside this lunar cycle, one for the season. Each is specific enough to fail, which is exactly what makes it worth keeping.

A plan you cannot break was never a plan.

Gate IV · the watchfire

What to be aware of

Your signature self-sabotage, the reasonable story it tells while it works, and the early warning sign that it has started again. Named once, it loses most of its cover.

The trap only works in the dark.

Gate V · the rise

How you rise to the Godly Form

The godly form in concrete detail, not as a halo but as habits. One keystone fear, one keystone practice, and a single sentence you carry out of the temple as a vow.

Same claws. Aimed.

行Chamber III · the five phases

Five fires tend this room

五行wǔ xíng, the five phases. Wood feeds fire, fire makes earth, earth bears metal, metal carries water, water grows wood.

Your animal burns in one of them, and the cycle says which phase feeds you and which one keeps you honest. Four celestial guardians hold the walls, the Azure Dragon in the east, the Vermilion Bird in the south, the White Tiger in the west, the Black Tortoise in the north.

Four stones keep every animal

Not ornaments. Offices. Each of the 144 animals names its own four keepers, one for each duty. Touch the office that calls you.

Moonlight is safe for every stone. Cleanse at the dark moon, charge at the full, dedicate at the new. Tradition of meaning, never medicine.

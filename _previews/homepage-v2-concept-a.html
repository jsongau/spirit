Every reading passes five gates.

One mechanism traced from where you stand to what you vow. Each gate is dated and falsifiable, and the last one ends in a practice, not a prophecy.

Where you stand now

An honest present-tense map, fallen or godly.

The need itself, not the craving that imitates it.

Three dated moves inside this lunar cycle and this season.

The keystone fear, the keystone practice, and a one-sentence vow.

Every animal holds two poles. The Fallen Form is ruled by its fear. The Godly Form is the same gifts, aimed.

Five phases, two motions.

Wood, fire, earth, metal, water. 五行wǔ xíng is one moving pattern: a cycle that feeds and a cycle that checks. Choose a motion, then touch a phase to read its plate.

Wood feeds fire. Fire makes earth of its ash. Earth bears metal. Metal carries water. Water raises wood.

Twelve palaces, fourteen stars.

The court astrology imperial China kept to itself for a thousand years: your life drawn as twelve rooms, with the stars stationed at the moment of your birth. BaZi tells you the weather. Zi Wei gives you the map.

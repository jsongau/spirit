The elements inside the element

What are hidden stems?

A single Earthly Branch is not one pure element. Each branch secretly holds one to three more elements inside it, called the hidden stems (藏干). So your animal's "main element" is really a small container of sub-elements. This hidden layer is where BaZi gets its depth: it roughly doubles what a chart carries, and it is what makes your animal feel bottomless.

Each branch ranks its interior by strength: a primary element (the branch's own), a middle element (what it is moving toward), and a residual (the season just past). Open any animal below and watch its inner elements appear.

Why it matters

The hidden layer does real work

It reveals what is concealed

A wealth star or a partner star can sit only in the hidden layer, invisible on the surface. Read just the four visible characters and you see about half the chart.

It roots the self

Your Day Master is "rooted" and strong when a branch hides its own element. Judging strength depends on counting that hidden support.

Where readers differ. The Horse 午 is given as 丁 alone by some, 丁 plus 己 by most, and the exact weightings are not standardized. We name the variation rather than assert one version.

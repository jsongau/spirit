Your Kua Number

Recorded as

The traditional Eight Mansions formula uses birth year and sex; some lineages read it differently, and it is one school among several.

The groups

East and West

Your Kua number, 命卦, places you in the East group, 東四命, with Kua 1, 3, 4, 9 and favorable directions north, south, east, and southeast; or the West group, 西四命, with Kua 2, 6, 7, 8 and favorable directions west, northwest, southwest, and northeast. The Kua uses the Chinese solar year that begins at the start of spring, 立春, so a birthday before early February uses the previous year, the same boundary our engine already handles.

Using it

Face a good direction

Everyone has four favorable directions, named 生氣, the strongest; 天醫, the heavenly doctor, a category name and not medical advice; 延年, for steadiness; and 伏位, for a settled foundation. Face one where it counts: the head of the bed, the seat at a desk, the way a main door faces. Schools differ on facing a direction versus pointing the head toward it, and Eight Mansions is one school among several.

寧níng靜jìng致zhì遠yuǎn

Literally through tranquility, reach the far

Meaning Only a calm, undistracted mind accomplishes far-reaching aims.

You cannot see far from a shaking place. Quiet the room and quiet the mind, and the horizon you were straining toward comes into view on its own.

A proverb we tie to the Year of the Snake. The pairing is our own reading, not tradition.
